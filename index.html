K.E.A. to Success


K.E.A. to Succes was founded in the summer of 2016 by three high school students. Co-founders Karena Blue, Emily Ojeda, and Alondra Gonzalez came together with one dream:to create a site that would help out any and everyone. With exactly one week to pitch, plan, create, and present their product, they came up with Project Help Me.
