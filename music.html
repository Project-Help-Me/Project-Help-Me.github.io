MUSIC

Where words fail, music speaks.

Have you ever been discourage to pursue something because of your age?

You're not alone! Don't feel like you can't do anything because you're far ahead in life. Many music artist started later in their career. Rachel Platten, Pharrell Williams, and Jay Z are all people who started their music careers at a late entry. Although, they started late they didn't let that set them back.

Jay Z

In the beginning of Jay's career he was selling cd's out his car. It wasn't until 1998 Jay Z had become famously known, and even then he was beginning to turn 30 years old. Jay Z first hit was when he was 28 and he's sold 100 million records (and received 21 Grammy Awards).

Sheryl Crow

Crow released her debut album at 31 and didn't get a massive hit until a year later with "All I Wanna Do." .The song was the winner of the 1995 Grammy Record of the Year and Best Female Pop Vocal Performance and was nominated for Song of the Year.

Rachel Platten

Platten had been making music since '03,it wasn't until 2015 she became famously known. At age 34 Platten just broke out this year in a major way (thanks in part to a Taylor Swift Instagram video of the ladies rocking out to Platten's song backstage) with "Fight Song.".
