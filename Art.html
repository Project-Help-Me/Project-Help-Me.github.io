Have you ever been discourage to pursue something because of your age?

You're not alone! Don't feel like you can't do anything because you're far ahead in life. Many artist started later in their career. Louise Bourgeois, Vincent Van Gogh, and Claude Monet are all people who started their art careers at a late entry. Although, they started late they didn't let that set them back.

Louise Bourgeois

Louise Bourgeois was a French-American painter. Bourgeois primarilly focuses on sculpting, which lead her to make the sculpture she is famoulsy known for her sculpture Spider. Bourgeois reached her greatest artistic success in her seventies and eighties.

Vincent Van Gogh

Van Gogh didn't actually start painting until he was in his late 20s. From then he created 2,100 pices of art in his entire life. He is most known for "The Starry Night"(which is shown above at the very top) and "Bedroom in Arles".

Claude Monet

Although Claude Monet had been making art since he was a toddler,Monet wasn't really known until after his wife's death when the artist was in his early 40s that he started creating the work for which he is most known. He is famously known for the painting "The Water Lily Pond"
