CONTINUING YOUR EDUCATION

It always seems impossible until it's done.

-Nelson Mandela

K.E.A. to Success is here to encourage you to continue your education career if that is what you are interested in. We want you to know that if you are considering on continuing your schooling, you definitely should. Go to college! Go to graduate school! Get your PhD! It is not too late to start. Many people later in life decide that they want to go back to school once their life is settled

Celebrities Who Have Accomplished Their Education Goals

James Franco was unsatisfied with his career and where it was headed when he decided to return to school in 2006. Franco went to school to complete his English degree. He later began taking multiple classes at graduate schools earning him his master's degree. Finally today Franco is working on his PhD and is a highly sought after university professor.

Steven Spielberg was rejected from his dream school, the University of Southern California (USC), because of his grades. He instead headed to California State University (CSU), but left because of a contract with Universal Studios. He was awarded an honorary degree from USC, but returned to CSU to complete his Bachelor's in English 35 years later.

America Ferrara (Ugly Betty) left college for her busy acting career. She decided to return 10 years later to complete her Bachelor's in International Relations.
