SPORTS

Winners never quit and quitters never win.

Have you ever been discourage to pursue something because of your age?

You're not alone! Don't feel like you can't do anything because you're far ahead in life. Many athletes started later in their career. Alex Morgan, Junior Don Santos, and Dikeme Mutombo are all people who started their athletic careers at a late entry. Although, they started late they didn't let that set them back.

Alex Morgan

Alex Morgan did not begin playing soccer until the age of 13, but she took to the game quickly and is now the premier striker for the USWNT. From then she became an Olympic gold medalist and FIFA Women's World Cup champion.

Junior Don Santos

Don Santos is a Brazilian mixed martial artist and former UFC Heavyweight Champion.He first began participating in martial arts at the age of 21, yet went professional within a year and he has since become one of the top MMA fighters in the world.

Dikembe Mutombo

Mutombo is a Congolese American retired professional basketball player who played 18 seasons in the National Basketball Association (NBA). He did not begin playing competitive basketball until he became a student at Georgetown University.
