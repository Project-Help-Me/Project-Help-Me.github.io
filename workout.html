The Workaholics Guide to Deskercise


Stuck at work all day? Why not spice things up a bit and do some deskercise. Yeah. That's right. Deskercise. Here some of our fave Deskercises:

The Twinkle Toe. Speedily tap your toes on the floor under your desk. To take it up a notch, stand in front of a small trashcan and lift those legs up to tap the toes on its edge, alternating feet.

Take the stairs. 'Nuf said.

Celebratory Split Squat Jumps. Figure out the solution to a major problem at work? Celebrate with the split squat jump. With both feet hip width apart, step the left leg back two feet and balance on the ball of the foot. Lower into a lunge and then burst upwards in an explosion of celebration. While in the air, switch feet so that the left foot is planted in front and the right leg is behind. Repeat 10-12 times for each side.

The Move and Shaker. Release stress and ignite the dance floor with a quick bout of seated dancing.

The Knuckle Sandwich. By far the best stress reliever on this list. Stand and throw a few jabs, hooks, ad uppercuts as fast as you can. Continue for a minute or longer to blow off steam and tone the arms, chest, and core.
